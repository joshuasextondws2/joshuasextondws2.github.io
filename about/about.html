About...

We are the space between two realities. In the town of Twin Peaks nothing is quite as it seems.. The Black Lodge/White Lodge is the embodyment of that. The douality of a craft coffee house and Cocktail lounge. Join us, Population 51,201.

We are Inspired by Twin Peaks which was created by David Lynch. We feel those sensibilities matched our in terms of terms of style and content. As passionate as we are about Twin Peaks we are just as passionate about coffee. We offer a large selection of dairy alternatives in order to really give customers plenty of options.

When we thought about what we really wanted to offer customers, we considered some of our favorite merch itmes. We love pins, mugs and tees. We hope to continue to offer you items that we are as passionate about as you. Thank You.
